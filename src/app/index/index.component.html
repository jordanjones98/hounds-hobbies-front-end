Burlington Hobbies

About

Burlington Hobbies has been in business since 2012, and has had an RC Race Track since 2014. Since opening the track has held over 100 events, and hosted over 1,000 racers.

Rookie Racers

Join us every Wednesday for rookie racers and get help from our experienced staff, and other drivers. Learn how to setup, tune, and drive your vehicle. After some practice we run a race with those who show up.

Friday Night Racing

Join us every Friday at 7 for a fun night of racing! Club racing is open to the public, and rental are available. Bring what you have, any classes with enough drivers will be ran.

Summer Series

Join us for our Summer Series starting June 6th! This will be a 12 race event where each position gets a certain amount of points. The driver with the most amount of points at the end of the series wins.
